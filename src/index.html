Welcome to the exciting world of Battleship! This iconic game has been a favorite of people around the world for decades, challenging players to use their strategic skills to sink their opponent's fleet of ships. Whether you're a seasoned player or new to the game, Battleship is easy to learn and offers endless possibilities for gameplay. Get ready to command your fleet, plot your attacks, and engage in an epic battle on the high seas!

    How to Play Battleship:
  1. Each player has a game board with two grids: one where they place their own ships and one where they keep track of their opponent's moves.
  2. Before the game begins, each player secretly places their ships on their grid. The ships can be placed horizontally or vertically, but not diagonally, and cannot overlap.
  3. Once the ships are placed, players take turns calling out a grid coordinate on their opponent's board, attempting to hit one of their opponent's ships.
  4. If a player successfully hits all of their opponent's ship's spaces, they win the game.
